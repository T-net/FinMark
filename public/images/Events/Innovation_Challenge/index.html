FMT Innovation challenge Why should I join the SADC Innovation challenge?

The SADC Innovation Challenge supports the design of innovative technological solutions that promote the use of and access to financial services in the region. You can choose to address innovative solutions in the following areas in financial inclusion:

  • SMME Financing
  • Digital Financial Identity
  • Women and Savings

Winners of the SADC Innovation Challenge will then further develop their solutions to be commercialised and operationalised in their respective countries.
